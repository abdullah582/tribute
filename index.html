Dr. Abd Al-Rahman bin Hamood Al-Sumait

He was known for his philanthropic works in more than 29 African countries.

Here's a time line of Al-Sumait's career

  • 1974-1976 Founding member of the Montreal branch of the Muslim Students Society.
  • 1980 Founding member, Malawi Muslims Committee.
  • 1981 – 1999 General Secretary of the African Muslims Committee

I was one of the orphans that Al-Sumait raised, helped, and contributed to their education
